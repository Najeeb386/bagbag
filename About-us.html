ABOUT-US

BagBag is an online shopping store which is successfully manufacturing and exporting leather products since 2012. We at BagBag are putting our utmost efforts in providing you high-quality and environmentally friendly leather products. We aimed to manufacture products that last longer without harming the environment. We are the manufactures and exporters of handbags, Foot wears, shoulder straps, wallets, jewelry, and other accessories. Our goal is to produce quality bags and wallets using innovative and sustainable materials such as Vegan leather. It is a type of leather made from pineapple leaves, recycled plastic, apple peels and other fruit waste.
